THE TEAM

We want to help people with visual impairments!

Visually impaired people cannot detect obstructions in front of them while traveling on the road (such as cars, ferocious dogs, pedestrians, traffic vehicles and other road-moving objects). Consequently, it is not timely (avoid cars, avoid hitting dogs, ...). Aims to increase initiative for the blind in social activities (detecting loved ones) and in walking on the road (detecting obstructions). We have a solution that combines hardware (raspberry pi) and software (object recognition algorithm YOLOv3) to detect obstructions and detect loved ones. This website aims to: Get the GPS data of the microprocessor device and display the coordinates of the device on a map with the Google Map API.
